GAME RULES

The game starts with a score of 10 points. You need guess the secret number. Enter a number from 1 to 20 and check. If the entered number is correct you win, whereas if it is incorrect you lose one point of your score. You can try as long as your score allows. When score becomes empty that is this round of the game is over. You can try again just click the "Again" button. Also you can see your highest score among all rounds of the game in "Highscore" line.
